## 3. Strategies for Interactive Pitching

### The "Check-In" Rule
Never speak for more than 3-4 minutes without a deliberate pause to verify understanding or alignment.
*   *Key Phrase:* "I want to pause there—how does that alignment with what you've seen in your department?"

### Bridge Questions
Use these to transition from your technical expertise back to the client's business reality:

1.  **The Bottleneck Bridge:** 
    *"Based on your current bottlenecks, where do you see the most potential for this solution?"*
2.  **The Perspective Bridge:** 
    *"I’ve shared a lot there—which of these three areas (e.g., Agents, Multimodality, or Reasoning) feels most relevant to your specific challenges?"*
3.  **The Roadmap Bridge:** 
    *"How does this change your perspective on your 2026 roadmap?"*
