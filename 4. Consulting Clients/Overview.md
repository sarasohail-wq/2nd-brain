# Consulting Methodology & Frameworks

## 1. The MECE Principle
**Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive.**
A fundamental way to group information. When solving a problem, ensure your categories don't overlap (ME) and that you've covered all possibilities (CE).

## 2. The Pyramid Principle (Barbara Minto)
*   **Answer First:** Start with the recommendation or conclusion.
*   **Group and Summarize:** Support your answer with logically grouped arguments.
*   **Logical Order:** Ensure your arguments follow a clear sequence (chronological, structural, or comparative).

## 3. The 80/20 Rule (Pareto Principle)
80% of the results come from 20% of the effort. In consulting, this means identifying the "critical few" issues that will have the biggest impact on the client's business.

## 4. Client Discovery Frameworks
*   **S.W.O.T:** Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats.
*   **P.E.S.T.E.L:** Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, Legal (used for market entry/macro analysis).

## 5. Value-Based Consulting
Moving away from "hours billed" to "value delivered." Focus on the ROI (Return on Investment) the client receives from the engagement.
